MY PROJECTS

STUDENTS ATTENDANCE TRACKER

The Student Attendance Tracker is a web-based application designed to streamline and automate the process of tracking attendance in educational institutions. This system allows teachers to efficiently record daily attendance, monitor student participation, and generate reports with ease. It reduces manual errors, provides real-time data, and offers a user-friendly interface for both educators and administrators. By integrating this system, institutions can enhance their record-keeping process, improve student accountability, and ensure accurate attendance tracking over time. This project showcases my ability to work with databases, front-end, and back-end technologies to solve real-world problems in the education sector.

CUSTOMER CHURN PREDICTION

The Customer Churn Prediction project aims to identify customers who are likely to stop using a product or service. By leveraging machine learning algorithms and analyzing historical data, this system predicts churn probabilities and provides insights into customer behavior. It helps businesses retain their customers by identifying at-risk individuals and enabling proactive measures, such as targeted marketing or personalized retention strategies. This project highlights my skills in data analysis, machine learning, and predictive modeling to solve critical business challenges and enhance customer retention.
